Features

Click an emote!

It will automatically be added to your message.

Click-to-use Emotes

With click-to-use emotes, it makes it super simple to use your favorite Twitch emotes (and some you didn't know about!).

Popularity helps!

We keep your most used emotes together.

Popular Emotes

Every time you use an emote, we keep track of how many times you used it which helps you not have to dig through and find your favorite emote.

Subscribe to everyone!

All your subscriber emotes will show in the menu.

Subscription Emotes

Do you subscribe to a load of people? Don't worry, we automatically grab your subscriber emotes and group them up for you.

Install instructions

  1. Install a userscript addon for your browser.
    • Firefox users: use Greasemonkey or Scriptish.
    • Chrome users: use Tampermonkey.
    • Opera users: use Tampermonkey for Opera.
  2. Click the install button below.
  3. Follow the instructions in the install window that pops up.
Install

Once installed, refresh the Twitch chat page and you should see a new button next to your chat box. Click that button to pop up the emote menu. Remember: responsibly.
